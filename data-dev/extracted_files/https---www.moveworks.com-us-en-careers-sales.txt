Marcello Gallo, Chief Revenue Officer
 Marcello brings more than 25 years of experience helping customers realize value through their journey of evaluating and adopting transformational, enterprise software solutions. Prior to joining Moveworks, Marcello held several sales leadership roles at MongoDB, BMC Software, and PTC.
Marcello holds a Computer Engineering Technology and Computer Science degree from Seneca College, where he graduated with High Honors.
The AI company that puts people first
                Moveworks is the AI platform that powers the world’s best places to work — but there’s nothing quite like working here. Every day, you’ll see the direct impact of your work on millions of people, at companies like DocuSign, Hearst, and Broadcom. You’ll have everything you need to do the most meaningful work of your career.
                        We’re in hyper-growth
Moveworks is one of the fastest growing companies on the planet. In 2016, Moveworks was four people in Mountain View. Now, it’s over 500 people around the world. With a $2.1 billion valuation, $315 million in total funding, we have no plans of slowing down any time soon.
                        Benefits
At Moveworks, we commit to taking care of our people — no matter their unique situation, needs, or location. That means we provide comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage, while ensuring that every employee around the globe has the same quality of healthcare.
