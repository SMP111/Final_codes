Generative AI at Work: crafting your enterprise copilot strategy
Oct 18, 2023 
 9:00am - 9:45am PT // 12:00pm - 12:45pm ET
From content generation to code writing, generative AI has become an indispensable tool for businesses looking to streamline processes and unlock new levels of efficiency. With a multitude of enterprise copilots in the market today, it's crucial to navigate these choices effectively. In this webinar, we’ll equip you with the knowledge and tools needed to realize the potential of generative AI via an enterprise copilot. You’ll learn:
How to differentiate various types of copilots and align their capabilities to your strategic objectives
What to consider when building requirements
How to deploy and evaluate the success of your copilot
