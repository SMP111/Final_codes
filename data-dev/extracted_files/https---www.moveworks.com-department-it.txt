Free up your help desk while delivering support at scale — Automatically
Help — right when your employees need it.
Employees expect help the moment they need it, wherever and whenever they ask. Moveworks responds to issues instantly, right on the chat platform your employees are already using.
Let your IT team focus on important projects.
Help your IT team make a bigger impact on the organization. Our AI chatbot handles routine requests and proactively solves potential issues, like reminding users to reset their passwords.
Get the most value from your resources.
Bring together all your forms, knowledge articles, and FAQs to one place. The Moveworks platform integrates with your systems to automatically deliver the right resources to your employees every time.

Never make your employees wait for support
To do their best work possible, your employees deserve support the moment they ask for it. With Moveworks, IT support issues, like account unlocks or software provisioning, won’t slow your team down. Our AI platform gives new hires access to the software they need on day one, while instantly responding to your team’s IT issues.
IT support that works as fast as you do
Your IT team should spend their time on high-impact projects that matter, like unlocking accounts and troubleshooting devices. Our AI chatbot enables self-service by automatically intercepting IT issues and resolving them right on your company’s chat platform. Every solution is relevant and secure because Moveworks understands each user’s role, location, and permissions.
Bring together all your resources in one place 
Your team spends a huge amount of time creating resources for employees to help themselves, from knowledge articles to forms to FAQs. Here’s the problem: Employees don’t know where to find them. Moveworks ensures that employees actually use your resources, giving them exactly the answers and forms they need directly on chat.

Palo Alto Networks needed a new approach to supporting productivity from anywhere. With a workforce of almost 15,000 people, its help desk had to deliver support on a much larger scale — without dramatically increasing its headcount.
