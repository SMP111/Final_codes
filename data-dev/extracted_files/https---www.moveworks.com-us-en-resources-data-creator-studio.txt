    What is Moveworks Creator Studio?
The modern enterprise typically deploys over 200 SaaS apps company-wide. This has led to a complex and fragmented experience for employees who are required to learn each of these applications just to get work done. All those investments in automations, data, and knowledge – yet employees don’t know how to reach them.
